• Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

    About Our Services

    What types of roofs do you install?

    We install a variety of materials including natural slate, ceramic tiles, asphalt, composite shingles and metal systems. Each material has its own advantages and we help you choose the best one for your property.

    How much does a new roof installation cost?

    The cost varies depending on size, accessibility, material type and condition of the underlying structure. Generally, it ranges between £45-65 per square meter. We offer quotes without obligation.

    How long does a complete installation take?

    A typical house (200m²) takes 3 to 7 days depending on weather, complexity and chosen material. Commercial projects may take longer.

    Do you offer emergency services?

    Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency service for leaks and roof damage. Call our emergency number for immediate assistance.

    About Warranties and Maintenance

    What warranty do you offer?

    We offer 3 years labour warranty on all our work. Materials come with manufacturer warranties that vary from 10 to 25 years depending on the product.

    What does the warranty cover?

    The warranty covers workmanship defects, leaks due to improper installation and structural failures. It does not cover damage from negligence, vandalism or extreme natural disasters.

    Is regular maintenance necessary?

    We recommend annual inspections and gutter cleaning twice a year. Preventive maintenance significantly extends the life of your roof.

    About Quotes and Payments

    How do I request a quote?

    You can request a quote through our online form, by phone or by visiting our offices. We will contact you within 24 hours to schedule an inspection.

    How much does the inspection cost?

    Quote inspections are completely free. Only if you proceed with the work is a detailed evaluation appointment charged (credited against the final quote).

    What are the payment methods?

    We accept bank transfer, credit/debit card, cheques and cash. A 30% deposit is required to confirm the date, with the balance due before completion.

    Do you offer financing?

    Yes, we can help you set up flexible payment plans for large projects. Contact us to discuss options.

    About Privacy and Security

    How do you protect my personal data?

    We use SSL encryption, restricted access and fully comply with GDPR. Your data is never sold to third parties. See our Privacy Policy.

    Do you use cookies?

    Yes, we use cookies to improve your experience on the site. You can control cookie preferences in your browser. See our Cookie Policy.

    What information do you ask for the quote?

    We need basic information: name, address, property type and project description. This information is used only to provide quotes and service.

    About Certifications and Safety

    Are you insured?

    Yes, we have R&C policy of £5 million. All our workers are insured and registered.

    What certifications do you have?

    We are members of NFRC (National Federation of Roofing Contractors), CTHC (Cheshire Trade) and comply with BS 1624 (British Standard).

    Do you do the work yourself or subcontract?

    We perform 95% of the work with our own team. For specific specialties, we work with trusted certified cooperators.
