    1. What are Cookies?

    Cookies are small text files stored on your browser or device when you visit a website. They are used to improve user experience, remember preferences and understand how you use our site.

    2. Types of Cookies We Use

    Essential Cookies

    Necessary for basic site functionality:

    • User session
    • Authentication and security
    • Shopping cart functionality (if applicable)

    You cannot opt out of these cookies.

    Analytics Cookies

    Help us understand how you use the site:

    • Google Analytics (tracking visited pages, session duration)
    • User behavior analysis
    • Identification of problematic pages

    Marketing Cookies

    Used for targeted advertising:

    • Facebook Pixel (for targeted ads)
    • Google Ads (conversion tracking)
    • Retargeting cookies

    Third-Party Cookies

    Set by external services:

    • Google Maps
    • Social networks (share buttons)
    • Embedded videos

    3. Cookie Banner

    We display a cookie consent banner on your first visit. You can:

    • Accept all: All cookies will be enabled
    • Reject non-essential: Only essential cookies
    • Customize: Select which cookies to accept

    4. Cookie Duration

    Cookie Type Duration
    Session Until browser closes
    Persistent Up to 2 years
    Analytics (Google) 26 months
    Marketing (Facebook) 90 days

    5. How to Control Cookies

    In your browser:

    • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
    • Firefox: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
    • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage cookies
    • Edge: Settings → Privacy → Cookies

    Note: Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

    6. Opt-Out Tools

    You can opt out of marketing cookies:

    • Google Data & Personalization
    • Facebook Ad Preferences
    • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI)

    7. Rights Under GDPR

    Under European privacy regulations:

    • You have the right to consent or reject non-essential cookies
    • You can change your preferences at any time
    • You can request we delete your cookie data
    • All consents are recorded and can be audited

    8. Analytics Services
