• Privacy Policy

    Last updated: February 2026

    1. Introduction

    Advanced Roof Line Cheshire Limited ("we", "our" or "the Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and protect your information.

    2. Information We Collect

    We collect information in the following ways:

    • Contact Information: Name, address, phone, email (when you request a quote or service)
    • Property Information: Property type, location, project details
    • Payment Information: Bank details, transaction data
    • Device Information: IP, browser, device type (through cookies)
    • Cookies and Similar Technologies: Browsing data and preferences

    3. How We Use Your Information

    We use your information to:

    • Process quotes and service requests
    • Deliver agreed services
    • Communications about your project
    • Billing and payment management
    • Service improvement
    • Marketing and promotions (with your consent)
    • Legal compliance

    4. Sharing Information

    We do not share your personal information with third parties, except:

    • Cooperators necessary to provide services
    • Payment processing providers
    • Legal authorities when required by law
    • With your explicit consent

    5. User Rights (GDPR)

    Under GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation), you have the right to:

    • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
    • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
    • Deletion: Request deletion of data ("right to be forgotten")
    • Portability: Receive data in structured format
    • Objection: Object to data processing

    To exercise these rights, contact privacy@advancedrooflinecheshire.co.uk

    6. Data Security

    We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

    • SSL/TLS encryption on all connections
    • Strong passwords in our database
    • Limited access to authorized personnel only
    • Regular security audits

    7. Data Retention

    We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to:

    • Provide requested services (6 years after completion)
    • Comply with legal obligations
    • Maintain work warranty (3 years)

    8. Cookies

    We use cookies to improve your experience. See our Cookie Policy for more details.
